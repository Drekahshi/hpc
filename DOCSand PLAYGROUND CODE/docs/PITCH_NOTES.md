### EPRA Hackathon Submission - Ready-to-Paste Sections
*(All sections under 500 characters)*

#### Problem Statement
Kenya's energy sector operates on blind institutional trust — KPLC billing, IPP contracts, and grid data are opaque, unverifiable, and inaccessible to communities and regulators alike. Rural CFAs and CBOs who steward renewable-rich land receive no value from the energy it generates. As AI infrastructure demand makes energy the new currency of intelligence, this transparency gap becomes a national equity crisis EPRA cannot regulate without verified data.

#### Proposed Approach
HISA People Chain deploys a Hedera-based trust layer across Kenya's energy sector — four pillars: JANI verifies community energy assets on-chain; CHAT logs grievances and outages immutably; UMOJA governs energy cooperatives democratically; HISA operates as an agentic AI layer that reads all pillars, generates policy intelligence for EPRA, mints carbon credit tokens, and routes verified value back to CFAs and CBOs who produce clean energy.

#### Innovation
HISA is the first agentic AI energy trust layer built on Hedera for African community governance. Unlike static reporting tools, HISA's AI agent continuously monitors verified on-chain energy data, identifies corruption patterns, scores communities for ESG-backed green finance, and advocates for energy's role as AI infrastructure — transforming EPRA from passive regulator into a real-time intelligence-driven institution powered by community data.

#### Data Sources
HISA integrates: Hedera Hashgraph public ledger for immutable records; Hedera Guardian for verified carbon credit and ESG data; KPLC grid and billing data via open API advocacy; EPRA regulatory filings; Kenya Forest Service CFA land registries; real-world CBO data from Oloolua, Karura, and Arabuko-Sokoke forests; satellite land-use data for asset verification; and community CHAT reports as ground-truth regulatory intelligence.

#### Expected Impact
HISA delivers: transparent energy pricing and IPP contract visibility for 5M+ Kenyan consumers; verified carbon credit income for 50+ CFAs and CBOs; tamper-proof grievance data empowering EPRA to act on corruption; democratic governance of community energy cooperatives; ESG impact scores unlocking green finance for rural producers; and a replicable continental model positioning Kenya as Africa's pioneer of equitable AI energy infrastructure governance.

#### Hackathon Feasibility
HISA People Chain has pre-deployed testnet tokens — JANI, UMOJA, CHAT, HISA — on Hedera, a live GitHub repository, and real CBO data from Oloolua Forest already tested on-chain. The Node.js/Express backend and Next.js frontend are production-ready. Within the hackathon window: JANI asset verification, CHAT grievance logging, and HISA agentic AI policy dashboard will be demonstrated live on Hedera testnet with authentic Kenyan community energy data.
